CheekyMonkey

Discription:

luxurious

Ingredients

  • bananas
  • dark chocolate
  • peanut butter
  • chiote
  • cayenne pepper
  • oat milk
  • coconut milk

Steps:

  1. in clean blender
  2. add 2 bananas
  3. add 2 1/2 cups oat milk
  4. add 2 tbsp cocnut milk
  5. add 1 tbsp dark chocolate
  6. add 2 tbsp peanut butter
  7. blend for 15 seconds
  8. add 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  9. add 1 tsp chiote
  10. blend for 1 minute or more

FountainOfYouth

Discription:

speaks to your DNA

Ingredients:

  • banana
  • strawberry
  • blueberry
  • raspberry
  • orange juice
  • coconut water
  • honey

Steps:

  1. in clean blender
  2. add 2 bananas
  3. add 1/2 cup strawberry
  4. add 1/2 cup blueberry
  5. add 1/2 cup raspberry
  6. add 2 cups orange juice
  7. add 1/2 cup coconut water
  8. drizzle in 1 tsp of honey
  9. blend for 1 minute of more
