Fountain of Youth

Discription:

rejuvenating mixed berry smoothie

Ingredients:

  1. banana
  2. strawberry
  3. blueberry
  4. raspberry
  5. orange juice
  6. coconut water
  7. honey

Steps:

  1. have blender or bullet cleaned and prepared for use
  2. add 2 bananas
  3. add 1/2 cup strawberry
  4. add 1/2 cup blueberry
  5. add 1/2 cup raspberry
  6. add 2 cups orange juice
  7. add 1/2 cup coconut water
  8. drizzle in 1 tsp of honey
  9. blend for 1 minute of more
