Cheeky Monkey

Discription:

deliciously rich, ice cold chocolate peanut butter smoothie

Ingredients

  • frozen bananas
  • dark chocolate
  • peanut butter
  • chiote
  • cayenne pepper
  • oat milk
  • coconut milk

Steps:

  1. have blender or bullet cleaned and prepared for use
  2. add 2 frozen bananas
  3. add 2 cups oat milk
  4. add 2 tbsp cocnut milk
  5. add 1 tbsp dark chocolate
  6. add 2 tbsp peanut butter
  7. blend for 15 seconds
  8. add 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  9. add 1 tsp chiote
  10. blend until desired texture has been reached
  11. enjoy
