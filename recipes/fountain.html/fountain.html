FOUNTAIN

DISCRIPTION:

foster youth and longevity with a potent blend of mixed berries

INGREDIENTS:

  • frozen banana
  • strawberry
  • raspberry
  • blueberry
  • blackberry
  • honey
  • ginger
  • fresh squeezed orange juice
  • coconut water

STEPS:

  1. place frozen 1 frozen banana into blender
  2. add 1/4 cup blueberries
  3. add 1/4 cup raspberries
  4. add 1/4 cup strawberries
  5. add 1/4 cup blackberries
  6. add 1 tbsp honey
  7. add 1 cup fresh squeezed orange juice
  8. add 1/2 cup coconut water
  9. add small pinch ginger
  10. blend until desired texture has been reached
