Discription:

taste of nature, green smoothie

Ingredients:

  • spirulina
  • banana
  • ground flax seed
  • blueberry
  • orange jucie
  • oat milk
  • coconut

Steps:

  1. have blender or bullet cleaned and prepared for use
  2. add 2 bananas
  3. add 1 cup blueberry
  4. add 1 cup orange juice
  5. add 2 cup oat milk
  6. add 1/4 cup shaved coconut
  7. add 1 tsp ground flax seed
  8. add 1 tbsp spirulina
