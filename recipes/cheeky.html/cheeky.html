CHEEKY

DESCRIPTION:

Decadent blend of dark chocolate joy

INGREDIENTS:

  • frozen banana
  • dark chocolate
  • peanut butter
  • coconut milk
  • oat milk
  • achiote
  • cinnamon
  • coffee

STEPS:

  1. add 3 frozen bananas
  2. add 1 tbsp dark chocolate
  3. add 1 tbsp peanut butter
  4. add 1/2 cup coconut milk
  5. add 2 cup oat milk
  6. add 1 tsp achiote
  7. add pinch of cinnamon
  8. add tsp ground coffee
