NATURE

DESCRIPTION:

a rejuvenating kiss of nature

INGREDIENTS:

  • frozen banana
  • blueberry
  • ground flax seed
  • almond milk
  • spirulina
  • cashew

STEPS:

  1. add 2-3 frozen banana
  2. add 1/2 blueberry
  3. add 2 cup alond milk
  4. add 1 tbsp ground flax seed
  5. add 1/4 cup cashew
  6. add tbsp spirulina
  7. blend
